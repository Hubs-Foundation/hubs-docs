Our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/hubs-498741086295031808) is the best way to quickly message us with your questions. Explore the Community and Support Forums to find the best channel for your question.

**Bug Reports and Discussions**

We track bugs and features on GitHub. You can [view existing tickets,](https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/hubs/issues) or open a [new issue or feature request](https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/hubs/issues/new/choose). If you would like to discuss a code-related topics with the Hubs team or the community you can browse our GitHub [discussion board](https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/hubs/discussions), or [start a new discussion](https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/hubs/discussions/new).
